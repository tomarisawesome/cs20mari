Mari's Musubaes

Mari’s Musubaes, established in 2025, is a small business dedicated to sharing the joy of musubi with our community. We’re passionate about creating simple, delicious food that brings people together, while celebrating both Japanese and American traditions.

Our Vision

We aim to become a go-to spot for fresh, high-quality musubis that everyone can enjoy. By blending cultural roots with modern flavors, we hope to make musubi a household favorite and a symbol of connection across different communities.

Our values

At the heart of Mari’s Musubaes are three values: quality, sustainability, and community. We source from local businesses, use fresh ingredients, and prioritize environmentally friendly practices. Most importantly, we want every musubi we serve to carry the warmth of care and culture.
